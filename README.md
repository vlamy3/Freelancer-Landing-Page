# Freelancer-Landing-Page

🌲 Freelancer Pro Template | Cedar & Code
Congratulations on investing in a high-converting home for your business! This template is designed specifically for freelancers, consultants, and service providers who want to turn visitors into paying clients.

📦 What’s in the Folder

• `index.html` - The main structure (Services, Pricing, FAQ).

• `style.css` - The "Business Vibe" styles + Dark Mode variables.

• `script.js` - Handles the Theme Toggle, Lucide icons, and Scroll Reveal.

🚀 Quick Start Guide

1. Customize Your Branding:

   • Open `style.css`.

   • Change the `--primary` variable to your brand color.

   • Update the `logo` text in `index.html`.

1. Edit Your Services:

   • Locate the `#services` section in `index.html`.

   • Swap out the icons using [Lucide.dev](https://lucide.dev/icons) names (e.g., replace `layout` with `camera` or `pen-tool`).

1. Update Your Pricing:

   • The middle card is the "Featured" card. Ensure this is your most profitable service!

1. Launch:

   • Drag and drop this entire folder into Netlify or Vercel to go live for free.

🌙 A Note on Dark Mode

This template uses `localStorage` to remember your visitor's preference. If they switch to Dark Mode, it will stay that way even if they refresh the page. This is a premium feature that makes your site feel custom-built.
